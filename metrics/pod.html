Photo on Delivery (POD)

Photo on Delivery (POD) is a critical operational metric that ensures delivery associates are taking correct photos of packages at the delivery location.

Achieving Compliance

There are different ways in which you can fail a photo on delivery

    Picture is Blurry
    Package is too close
    Human In Picture
    Picture is Dark
    Package in Hand
    No Package Detected
